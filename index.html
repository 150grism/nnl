How to get started with Qpower?
First go to the Sign Up page, enter your information, and submit the form. After that you`ll get the secret code via email for account activation. Please note: After the account activation, check your email again to receive from us the important information about your account. Then you need to verify your account to enable bank deposits and withdrawals. For this go to Verification page. After you`ve added funds to your account, you are ready to trade.
